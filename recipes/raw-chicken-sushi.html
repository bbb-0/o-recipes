Raw Chicken Sashimi

Deliscious delicate raw chicken will aboslutely not melt in your mouth and will have a high probability of giving solmonella

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts, bone and skin removed
  • Ponzu sauce
  • Daikon

Cooking directions

  1. Slice chicken breasts into thin pieces and serve on plate
  2. Finely slice daikon for garnish
  3. Serve Ponzu sauce seperately for preference
