Chicken marsala

Chicken marsala is an Italian-style recipe for tender pan fried chicken breast in a sweet marsala wine and mushroom sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour for coating
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 4 chicken breasts, bone and skin removed - pounded 1/4 inch thick
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 marsala wine
  • 1/4 cup cooking sherry

Cooking directions

  1. In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano.
  2. Coat chicken pieces in the flour mixture.
  3. In a large skillet melt butter in oil over medium heat.
  4. Place chicken in the pan, and lightly brown.
  5. Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms.
  6. Pour in wine and sherry.
  7. Cover skillet; simmer chicken for 10 minutes, turning once, until no longer pink and juices run clear.
